Research and Projects

My research interests are in Quantum Information, Quantum Optics and Differential Geometry.

Institute for Quantum Computing - Undergraduate Research Assissant

Adaptive Optics for Addressing Ions in an Ion Trap.

Details

We implement adaptive optics in a laboratory setting with the goal of gaining optical abberation correction and detailed, in-situ and real-time addressing of ions in a Yb+ ion trap. In particular, I am working on increasing the power efficiency of the method.

pySLM: Python Spatial Light Modulation

Github PyPI

A python package for controlling spatial light modulators, such as LCD adaptive optics or Digital Micromirror devices. Co-authored by myself and Gilbert Shih.
